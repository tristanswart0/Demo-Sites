Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Avery Miles and Jordan Clarke, SparkBright Electrical began with a single van and a promise: do every job better than expected. Our first project—restoring a storm-damaged café—sparked a reputation for reliability and care. Today, our team of eight licensed electricians brings that same dedication to homes and businesses across the region.

We’re passionate about clean work, clear communication, and making every client feel like our only client. From smart home upgrades to emergency repairs, we’re here to help you live and work brighter.
