Our Story

In 2015, co-founders Avery Miles and Jordan Clarke launched SparkBright Electrical from a single work van and a shared belief: do every job better than expected. Their first client was a café owner, Marcus Hale, whose kitchen rebuild after storm damage made SparkBright a trusted local name.

Today, SparkBright is a team of eight electricians blending old-school craft with smart home and energy-efficient technology.
